True Potential

Cole is the third to find his true potential. He does so by mending his broken relationship with his dad. Cole has been pretending to be at dance school for the duration of his ninja escapades. Cole's dad is the most talented dancer in all ninjago and wants his son to follow in his footsteps. The ninja were chasing a macguffin called a fang blade and they had to dance to get it. As they were about to get it, the serpentine attack Cole's father as a distraction to get the fangblade. Cole runs to save his father and becomes imbued with the strength of the earth.
