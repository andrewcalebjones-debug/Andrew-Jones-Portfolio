True Potential

Zane is the first to discover his true potential. He does so by coming to a startling revelation about his humanity - he is really a robot. Zane remembers being an orphan all his life and has no idea where he came from. One day, Zane follows a curious acting falcon to the icy clacier barrens where he finds a workshop in hollowed out tree. Here, he finds his own blueprints. The other three ninja try to comfort him, but Zane's grief is overwhelming, so they leave him alone. Zane finds a switch in his stomach that shows him his past. It shows his father raising Zane to protect the defenseless and dying in his old age. As he drifted off he flicked Zane's memory switch which left Zane with no recollection of his father or his upbringing. Zane is invigorated at finding his memories and steps outside only to see his friends being pulverized by giant "treehorns." Zane beams with light and freezes their queen, saving his brothers.
