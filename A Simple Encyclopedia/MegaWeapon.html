History

This weapon was forged from the golden weapons of spintitzu by Lord Garmadon after defeating the Great Devourer. Garmadon was given the weapons by the ninja to complete this task, but was told to bring them back. Garmadon abused the ninjas' trust and formed the Mega Weapon. This starts a series of incedents where Garmadon creates some evil force to stop the ninja but is foiled every time. It ends when Garmadon takes the ninja into the past to stop Lloyd's training. The ninja find the intact golden weapons and use them to destroy the Mega Weapon (This actually results in the past weapons and the Mega Weapon being sent into space in a gold lump)
