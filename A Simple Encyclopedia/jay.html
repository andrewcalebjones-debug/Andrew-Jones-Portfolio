True Potential

Jay is the second ninja to unlock his true potential. He does so by realizing that his worth cannot be found in pretending to be someone else. He and Nya start dating but, due to the serpentine's evil plan, find themselves tied to a runaway rollercoaster headed for certain doom. In the moments before it crashes, Nya tells jay that he doesn't need to pretend to be someone else for her to love him. "You are the best you," she says and kisses him. Jay stands up and becomes one with lightning and stopped the runaway rollercoaster.
