About Us

Arbor Oaks has had a long, rich history much of which is
intertwined with the nearby higher education insitution,
Emmaus University.

The following describes our history and core beliefs.

A Brief History

Arbor Oaks Bible Chapel began with the move of Emmaus Bible College (EBC) from Chicago to Dubuque, Iowa in 1984. In early 1984 the faculty and staff of EBC agreed to establish a committee to make sure an assembly meeting be ready in time for the transfer of the college to Dubuque later in the year. The college was to be located at Asbury Road and the assembly was to meet at the college facility and called Asbury Road Bible Chapel. The first meeting was held June 17, 1984. From that early meeting another assembly, Asbury Community Chapel was established in the adjacent city of Asbury. Early in the 1990’s the fellowship decided to purchase some land to potentially build a chapel. A 6 acre parcel of land was purchased on JFK Road, about 1½ miles from Emmaus Bible College. In 2002 it was decided to construct a chapel building. A building committee was established and church designs reviewed and construction quotes obtained. A design was agreed upon and final quotes obtained, with construction starting late in 2002. In 2003 the building was completed and furnished.

Our Core Beliefs

  • We believe that the Bible is God's Word.
  • We believe in one God.
  • We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ is God.
  • We believe in the person and work of the Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the sinfulness of human nature.
  • We believe that Jesus Christ paid the penalty for mankind's sin.
  • We believe in the second coming of Christ and His subsequent millennial reign upon the earth.
  • We believe in the future resurrection of all mankind and in the last judgment of the unsaved.
  • We believe that the term Marriage has only one meaning the uniting of one man and one woman in a single, exclusive union, as delineated in Scripture.
