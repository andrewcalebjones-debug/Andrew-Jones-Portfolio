Arbor Oaks Bible Chapel

An Evangelical, Open Brethren Assembly

We believe that the program of God in this age is to build the church of Jesus Christ.

Beginning on the day of Pentecost, all believers in Christ are brought into the church, the body of Christ, and are commissioned to proclaim the gospel worldwide. This local congregation is a small part of that great body and functions autonomously, with Christ as its head governing through a plurality of elders. God has established two church ordinances: believer’s baptism and communion. These are to be exercised within the essential functions of the local church; namely, teaching, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayer.
